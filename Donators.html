Frequently Asked Questions

How do I claim my perks after donating? ▼
Open a ticket in the discord and any available staff will be there to assist. This is a manual process and can take some time.
Do perks survive a server wipe? ▼
Yes! Your "Donator Status" and associated perks (like the chat prefix, increased limits, and kit access) persist through server wipes for the duration of your chosen tier.
Can I upgrade my tier later? ▼
This can make things complicated . . . When you open your ticket, please mention this.
What happens if I get banned? ▼
Donator perks do not grant immunity from server rules. If you are banned for cheating, scripting, or severe toxicity, your contribution will not be refunded. We keep the environment fair for everyone.

Security Warning

As mentioned above, this is just to support the server and get some added perks. We will NEVER contact you asking you for money to play on our server. If you are contacted in-game or on Discord by anyone claiming to be staff requesting money to play, please report them immediately and block them. This is a scam attempt. We do not charge to play on this server.
